# 📘 Case Study: Integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Internet of Things (IoT) in Smart Healthcare

## 📖 Overview

This repository contains a comprehensive case study on the integration of **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** and the **Internet of Things (IoT)** in the healthcare sector, focusing on an **Intelligent Remote Patient Monitoring System**.
The case study explores how IoT-enabled wearable devices collect real-time patient health data and how AI algorithms analyze this data to detect anomalies, predict potential health risks, and provide timely alerts to healthcare professionals.
This report was prepared as part of my internship assignment to demonstrate the practical application of AI and IoT technologies in solving real-world healthcare challenges.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Understand the integration of AI and IoT technologies.
- Study the architecture of an intelligent healthcare monitoring system.
- Analyze the benefits and challenges of AI-powered IoT solutions.
- Explore future advancements in smart healthcare.

## 🏥 Case Study Highlights

The proposed healthcare system consists of:

- 📡 IoT wearable sensors for collecting patient vital signs
- 📶 ESP32 for wireless communication
- ☁️ Cloud platform for secure data storage
- 🤖 AI algorithms for health analysis and anomaly detection
- 📱 Doctor dashboard/mobile application for real-time monitoring
- 🚨 Automatic emergency alerts for abnormal health conditions
